Overview

Circuit is a six-sprint accelerator programme for Southeast Asian Web3 founders, organised by NewCampus in partnership with Base (Coinbase's Layer 2 blockchain network). This agreement governs how personal data, business information, and application content submitted to Circuit is collected, stored, used, and shared.

This page covers two participant groups. Use the toggle below to highlight the terms most relevant to you — or read the full document for a complete picture.

Before you apply

Please read this document carefully before submitting your application. By proceeding, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to the terms set out here.

02

Who We Are

Circuit is operated by NewCampus ('we', 'us', 'our'). NewCampus is the named organiser of the programme and the data controller responsible for all personal data collected through Circuit's application process and programme operations.

Base (Coinbase) participates as a technology and ecosystem partner. Base personnel will have access to certain application data for the purposes of programme review and founder evaluation, as described in this agreement.

Important

NewCampus is the sole data controller. Base is a data recipient acting in an advisory and evaluation capacity. Neither party will sell, rent, or commercially exploit participant data.

04

Founder Terms

4.1 Stage 1 — Expression of Interest (EOI)

At the EOI stage, we collect only what is needed to assess your fit for the programme:

  • Identity: full name, role, email address, company name, country of incorporation
  • Online presence: LinkedIn profile, GitHub handle, X/Twitter and other social links
  • Product information: one-liner, current stage, sector, blockchain network
  • Traction: qualitative description of community or user base; fundraising and TGE intentions
  • Residency commitment: availability and ability to self-fund the in-person residency week
  • Video (optional): a short intro video submitted via link (e.g. Loom)
  • Fit: reasons for applying to Circuit; referral source

EOI data is visible to Base and NewCampus only. It will not be shared with other founders, investors, or third parties.

4.2 Stage 2 — Full Application (Invited Applicants Only)

Founders who are shortlisted will be invited to complete a full application. This stage collects more detailed information, including financial data, cap table, fundraising details, token and TGE plans, and team information.

Explicit consent required

Stage 2 data will not be collected without your explicit, named, and timestamped consent. A consent declaration will be presented at the point of submission. You may withdraw from the process at any time before submitting.

4.3 Data Room Access

Upon acceptance into Circuit Cohort 1, a curated founder profile will be made available to invited investors via the Circuit Data Room. This profile is derived from your application and will include your company name, sector, product description, stage, traction summary, fundraising ask, and team overview.

You will be informed before your profile is made visible in the data room, and you will have the opportunity to review and approve your profile content prior to publication.

05

Investor Terms

5.1 Application Data

Investors applying to Circuit submit identity and professional information, investment profile details (AUM, cheque size, stage focus, sector preferences), geographic focus, Web3 investment experience, and logistics availability. Investor application data is visible to Base and NewCampus only — never to founders or other investors.

5.2 Data Room Access & Non-Disclosure Obligation

Non-disclosure obligation

All information in the Circuit Data Room — including founder profiles, financial data, cap tables, and fundraising details — is strictly confidential. By accessing the data room, you agree not to share, forward, screenshot, reproduce, or otherwise disclose this information to any third party without the prior written consent of NewCampus and the relevant founder.

Specifically, investors agree that they will not:

  • Share data room content with colleagues, partners, or other investors not approved by Circuit
  • Use data room information for purposes other than evaluating investment opportunities within Circuit
  • Contact founders directly using information obtained from the data room without Circuit's facilitation
  • Retain copies of data room content beyond the programme duration without the founder's explicit consent

A separate click-wrap confirmation of these obligations will be presented at the point of data room access. Access is not granted until this confirmation is completed.

5.3 Expressed Interest

Investors may indicate interest in specific founders through the Circuit platform. These expressions of interest are visible to Base and NewCampus only, not visible to other investors, and not disclosed to founders until Circuit facilitates a formal introduction.

06

How We Use Your Data

Data collected through Circuit is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Programme administration — reviewing applications, shortlisting candidates, managing cohort operations
  • Evaluation — enabling Base and NewCampus to assess founder and investor fit
  • Matching — facilitating introductions between accepted founders and approved investors
  • Communication — programme updates, scheduling, and feedback

We do not use participant data for marketing, advertising, commercial profiling, data brokering, or training AI or machine learning models.

07

Data Retention

EOI submissions (unsuccessful) Deleted or anonymised within 12 months
Stage 2 applications (unsuccessful) Deleted or anonymised within 12 months of outcome notification
Accepted founder data Retained for programme duration + up to 24 months for alumni tracking
Investor data Retained while engaged across cohorts, or up to 24 months from last engagement

You may request deletion of your data at any time. Requests will be processed within 30 days.

08

Your Rights

Under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) 2012, you have the right to:

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction

Request that inaccurate or incomplete data be corrected.

Withdrawal

Withdraw consent to data processing at any time, without affecting prior processing.

Deletion

Request that your data be deleted, subject to any legal retention obligations.

Portability

Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Objection

Object to processing of your data for specific purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, contact the Circuit programme team at the email address provided in your application confirmation.

09

Security

  • Access controls — programme data is accessible only to authorised Base and NewCampus personnel on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure hosting — all application and data room infrastructure is hosted on platforms with industry-standard security certifications
  • No public exposure — no participant data is published publicly or indexed by search engines

In the event of a data breach likely to affect your rights or interests, we will notify you as soon as practicable and in accordance with applicable law.
